# Veil of the Hearthstar — World Bible (Phase 1)

## 1) Original Title
**Veil of the Hearthstar**

## 2) World / Kingdom Names
- **Continent:** Caldrayne Reach
- **Ancient Kingdom (fallen):** Solvharrow Crown
- **Current fractured realms:**
  - Tidelan Marches
  - Briarward Communion
  - Cindervale Holds

## 3) Protagonist
**Name:** Cael Thorne

A young oath-runner of the Lantern Wardens who carries a broken family shield called **Dawnback** and an heirloom blade, **Rillfang**. Cael begins as a courier who survives by grit and caution, then grows into the bearer of the Hearthstar line.

Silhouette:
- split mantle cloak (storm-blue outer, ember lining)
- triangular kite shield
- long one-handed blade + relic satchel on hip

## 4) Main Threat
**Primary Antagonist:** **Aureth Khar**, the Winged Ash Monarch.

Aureth was the final sky-marshal of Solvharrow. He attempted to save the kingdom by binding the memory of the dead into ash-fire engines. The rite failed. Now he devours memory to hold form, spreading the **Sootwake Veil** that erases names, roads, and hope.

## 5) Four Main Dungeons
1. **Roothush Reliquary** (overgrown sanctuary)
   - Mechanic: chain-linked seed anchors (weight + momentum puzzles)
   - Major item: **Thornchain Grapnel**
   - Boss: **Saint Veyru, Blind of Stone**

2. **The Bellglass Aerie** (sky observatory ruins)
   - Mechanic: wind turbines + resonant bell channels
   - Major item: **Aeolune Whistle** (wind command relic)
   - Boss: **Kezhal, Serpent of Stormglass**

3. **Noctmere Oathvault** (drowned crypt and black reservoir)
   - Mechanic: waterline shifting + moon-reflection mirrors
   - Major item: **Moonwake Lantern**
   - Boss: **Sir Edrin Drownbound**

4. **Pyre-Crown Crucible** (volcanic forge cathedral)
   - Mechanic: heat routing + molten gate pressure
   - Major item: **Ember Sigil**
   - Boss: **Varkhonn the Memory-Smelter**

Late-game final fortress:
- **Cairn of Last Cinders** (corrupted capital-citadel)

## 7) Core Progression Items
- **Rillfang** (base sword; receives 3 tempered upgrades)
- **Dawnback Shield** (block + timed deflect)
- **Thornchain Grapnel** (pull rings, yank shields, cross gaps)
- **Aeolune Whistle** (spin vanes, create lift gusts, disrupt flyers)
- **Moonwake Lantern** (reveal hidden bridges/inscriptions)
- **Ember Sigil** (ignite braziers, charge forges, burn rot barriers)
- **Runic Bow of Vales** (utility ranged shots)
- **Anchor Gauntlet** (late act: move sacred mass locks)

## 8) Factions
- **Lantern Wardens** (rangers + oathkeepers)
- **Scriptorium of Irix** (scholars and cartographers)
- **Bastion Clans of Keldran** (mountain smith-families)
- **River Guild of Rillwatch** (merchant flotillas)
- **Choir of Hollow Ash** (cult serving Aureth)

## 9) Story Arc (Acts)
- **Act 1:** Prologue collapse at Starwell Grove, Cael inherits Hearthstar shard.
- **Act 2:** Open exploration; clear Roothush + Bellglass dungeons.
- **Act 3:** Betrayal by a senior Warden revealed as Choir agent; clear Noctmere + Pyre-Crown; tragic reveal that old heroes caused the first veil fracture.
- **Act 4:** Settlements darken, citizens forget loved ones; assault Cairn of Last Cinders.
- **Ending:** Cael restores true names to the kingdom but gives up personal claim to the crown, choosing to rebuild as a Warden among people.

## Emotional Tone
Hopeful melancholy; noble speech without excess; warmth through found-family relationships and settlement renewal.
